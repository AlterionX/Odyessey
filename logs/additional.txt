Chat loc (.oclog) format:

//Header:
CONVO_NAME
Speakers:SPEAKER_NAME1\tSPEAKER_NAME2
DEFAULT_DELAY
//Body (repests):
DELAY_TIME:SPEAKER_NUM:CONVO_LINE //if left blank, is a system message


History log (.llst) format:
//Body (repeats):
LOG_FILE_NAME

Category (.oplist) format:
//Body (repeats):
OPTION_STRING:OPTION_FILE_NAME

Option (.option) format:
//Body (repeats):
LOG_FILE_NAME
